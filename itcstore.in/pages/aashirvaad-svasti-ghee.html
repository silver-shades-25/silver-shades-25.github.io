Home Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee
Explore the new range of Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee View all

Aashirvaad Svasti is a Pure Cow Ghee that promises impeccable taste and aroma. Made with special Slo Cook Process, Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee is prepared by cooking slowly for about three and half hours over gradually increasing temperature which is turn enchances it's natural aroma and taste.

Available in multiple pack types and sizes, Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee lends itself beautifully to varied Indian cuisines.

Why is Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee a must-try?

Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee Recipes

Gajar ka Halwa

Amongst all sweet recipes Gajar ka Halwa is a must try! It is an absolute delight the winter months. This warm, sweet and rich dessert is a perfect end to any meal.
